Case Study 1 - Technical SEO - On-Page

SEO Optimisation of My Digital Marketing Portfolio Website

Overview

This project focused on improving the search visibility and overall structure of my digital marketing portfolio website. The goal was to apply practical SEO techniques to optimise the site for relevant keywords and improve its discoverability through search engines.

Objectives

  • Improve the SEO structure of the website
  • Optimise page titles and meta descriptions
  • Apply keyword research for relevant search terms
  • Improve content structure and internal linking

Tools Used

Google Search Console SEMrush Ahrefs Wix SEO Tools

Strategy and Implementation

  1. Keyword Research - Conducted keyword research to identify relevant search terms related to digital marketing portfolios and SEO projects. Target keywords included: digital marketing portfolio, SEO portfolio, digital marketing projects.
  2. On-Page SEO Optimisation - Optimised page titles, meta descriptions, headings (H1/H2 structure), internal links and keyword placement across the site.
  3. Content Optimisation - Updated homepage and About page content to include relevant SEO keywords while keeping content natural and user-friendly. Added structured sections including skills, featured projects and certifications.
  4. Technical SEO Improvements - Improved sitemap submission, page structure, image alt text and ensured a mobile-friendly layout.

Outcome

This project provided practical experience in implementing SEO strategies and improving website structure for search engines. Developed hands-on skills in keyword research, on-page SEO optimisation and content structuring. Key learnings included how search engines evaluate page titles, the importance of structured headings and internal linking, and practical application of SEO tools and analytics platforms.

Digital Marketing Strategy for ASOS

Overview

This academic project involved developing a digital marketing strategy for ASOS with a focus on promoting sustainable fashion through digital platforms.

Objectives

  • Develop a digital marketing strategy to promote sustainable fashion
  • Increase brand engagement through social media campaigns
  • Explore influencer marketing strategies
  • Deliver measurable improvements in traffic and engagement metrics

Tools Used

Google Analytics 4 HubSpot SEMrush

Strategy and Implementation

  • Market Research - Analysed current digital marketing trends within the fashion industry and evaluated how brands communicate sustainability initiatives to consumers.
  • Audience Analysis - Examined consumer behaviour patterns and identified target audiences interested in sustainable fashion and ethical consumption.
  • Social Media Campaign Strategy - Developed a social media campaign focusing on influencer collaborations, interactive content and storytelling around sustainability.

Key learnings included the importance of consumer insights, the role of influencers in brand communication and strategic content planning for digital campaigns.
